Space Adventure VR Manual

Student: Shaurya Dewani

Course: CMPT 461 Immersive Computing

Project: Individual Project based on A-Frame

How to Run

Open index.html directly, or run a local static server for best compatibility.

Controls

  • Look around to steer.
  • Forward motion is automatic during gameplay.
  • Gaze at menu buttons to activate them.

Objectives

  • Level 1: Collect at least 8 glowing rings (+10 each), avoid asteroids, then reach the portal.
  • Level 2: Collect at least 7 crystals (+20 each) in a narrow tunnel, avoid barriers and wall contact, then reach the final exit.

Music Toggle

Use the setup menu button to toggle background music ON/OFF.

HUD and Scoring

Score and health HUD are always visible during active gameplay (both levels).

Deployment

Deploy as a static site via GitHub Pages from repository root branch.

See README.md for complete setup and deployment details.
